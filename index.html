About

Hello! My name is Rose Fitzgerald, and I am a senior majoring in Marketing and Business Analytics at the University of Iowa - Tippie College of Business. Through my diverse work experiences, I have developed essential skills in leadership, effective communication, time management, and teamwork. These experiences include serving as a Customer Service Specialist at Turck, an Admissions Office Receptionist at the University of Iowa, a Rotational Development Intern at Principal Financial, and a participant in the Insure Your Future Program (Summer 2024). As I approach graduation in May 2025, I am seeking a career that offers continuous learning, professional development, and the opportunity to make a meaningful impact. I am open to exploring various industries and locations. I am eager to contribute to an organization where I know my work is making a difference and I thrive in environments that challenge me to grow and encourage innovation. I would love to connect and explore opportunities that align with my passion for learning and creating positive change!
